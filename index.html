About Me

As one that constantly looks for ways to reinvent and improve, Melissa made the decision to change careers from non-profit/social service to Information Technology. Initially, learning to code was a way to add skills and remain competitive. However, it quickly turned into her primary interest and she began using online tutorials to learn. Currently she is a Software Engineering Apprentice with We Can Code IT boot camp in Cleveland, Ohio.

Born and raised in Cleveland, she has lived in New Orleans, Louisiana, Gettysburg, Pennsylvania and Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. When not creating sites and programs, Melissa is focused on her first love - travel. Her two favorite places are Toronto, Canada and Paris, France and she looks forward to having a vacation home in both locations.
